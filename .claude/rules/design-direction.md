# Design Direction

## Color & Theme

- Dark background, light text
- Cinematic palette — deep blacks, warm highlights, muted accents
- High contrast for readability; subtle gradients for depth
- Accent (yellow) buttons always use white text (`--color-text-primary`), never dark

## Typography

- Clean sans-serif typeface (e.g., Inter, Outfit, or similar)
- Dramatic scale contrast between headings and body text
- Generous line-height and letter-spacing for elegance

## Layout

- Full-viewport hero sections
- Grid-based portfolio layout (responsive, 1–3 columns)
- Generous whitespace — let visuals breathe
- Mobile-first, fully responsive

## Animation

Current phase: **experimentation** — try things, iterate freely.

Guidelines:
- Smooth page/section transitions
- Scroll-triggered reveals (fade-in, slide-up)
- Hover effects on portfolio cards (scale, overlay, parallax)
- Keep animations purposeful — enhance, don't distract
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: disable or reduce all motion

## Media

- No autoplay video or music
- Optimise images (WebP/AVIF, lazy loading, srcset)
- Use poster frames for video thumbnails
